Stark Tech aims to establish a high-tech Greenfield dairy unit in the outskirts of Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. The enterprise will focus on the procurement, chilling, and distribution of high-quality pasteurized milk and dairy-related services. Unlike traditional dairies, Stark Tech will utilize technology to track milk quality and automate the supply chain, ensuring "farm-to-table" freshness within 12 hours.

The business will also invest in cold-chain logistics (refrigerated vans) to maintain the integrity of the product. The target market includes the burgeoning residential complexes in Lucknow, local tea-stall chains, and organic food retailers. By focusing on unadulterated, tech-monitored milk production, the enterprise aims to capture a niche segment of health-conscious consumers in the state capital.
